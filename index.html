About Us
We are a community driven website centred around the National College of Ireland. Here you can find useful information regarding the college along with information about various other events being held by or regarding NCI students.
Covid Info
Unfortunately, due the Covid 19 pandemic the majority of classes are being held online with only some classes actually being held in person on campus. Covid 19 mask and social distancing regulations are still in place and are mandatory if attending campus for any in person class. In person class will still be available online for any students not comfortable coming in.
Exam Info
Exam season is coming up and the NCI support and info community site is a great way of finding information about upcoming exams. We have provided links to the January exam timetable for each year and module. If you need any more information about the exams don’t hesitate to get in contact with us.

Lecture Info
Any students the need information or have questions about attending their lectures in person or online can feel free to reach out to us and we will try help you get queries sorted.
Library Info
Having issues regarding the library? Don’t worry we can help. We can provide you with the corrected information about the library opening hours and up to date information regarding its restrictions due to covid 19.
